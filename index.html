Calum Scott

Singer and songwriter

I was born and raised in the city of Hull, in England

I was initially a drummer before being persuaded to sing by my sister Jade, herself a brilliant vocalist.

You may know me from my audition for season nine of Britain’s Got Talent, where I performed a cover of Robyn's Dancing on My Own. Following a standing ovation from the judging panel, Simon Cowell pressed the Golden Buzzer giving me an automatic place in the live shows. Explaining his decision, Cowell said: I've never ever in all the years I've done this show heard a guy with the talent you've got. Seriously. And the version was sensational, and that shows to me that you're more than a singer, you're an artist and that's why you got that (the buzzer). Read more here...

You Are The Reason started out as a song about something completely different than what it is now. It was originally written from a fragile place of anxiety and not knowing the cause of it, so the song started to become Without A Reason. But as I carried on writing, I realised how every relationship has difficult times but you stick with it because the love you fundamentally feel for the other person overrides everything else. I wrote this song to say thank you to those special people who make our lives better, simply because they’re in it. Read more here...
